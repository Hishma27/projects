POPULAR

There are many popular places around the world
Some of popular places of different countries are

India-Taj Mahal

An immense mausoleum of white marble, built in Agra between 1631 and 1648 by order of the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his favourite wife.

Dubai-Burj Khalifa

It is an skyscraper in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It is the world's tallest building with a total height of 829.8m(2,722ft). It is the tallest building in the world.

Turkey-Bosphorus Bridge

Its is famous for its important transport routes, connceting parts of Europe to Turkey. This bridge is 1560m long and fourth largest suspension bridge in the world.

Canada-Niagara Falls

It is a group of three Waterfalls at the southern end of Niagara Gorge, spanning the border between the province of Ontario in Canada and state of NewYork in United States. Its the combination of height and volume that makes Niagara Falls so breathtaking.

Egypt-Pyramids of Giza

It is the largest Egyptian pyramid and served as the Tomb pf Pharaoh Khufu, who ruled during the fourth dynasty of the Old Kingdom.

China-The Great Wall Of China

It is one of the seven construction wonders in the world not only for its long history, but its massive construction size, and its unique architectural style.
